# Trademarks and brand

This document covers brand use under the source-available license and records a
preliminary trademark freedom-to-operate assessment. **It is not legal advice.**

The product is named **StrataGraph** (domain `stratagraph.dev`).

## Brand-use policy

The Functional Source License ([LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md)) grants broad rights to
the **code** for any non-competing purpose. It does not grant rights to the **name or
brand**:

- "StrataGraph", `stratagraph`, and the StrataGraph logo are trademarks of the
  licensor.
- You may fork and redistribute the source under the FSL, but you may not use
  the marks to name or promote your fork or any derivative as if it were the
  official project. Name your fork something else.
- Only official, project-signed releases may use the marks.
- Nominative use (truthfully referring to StrataGraph, for example "compatible
  with StrataGraph") is fine.

## Why "StrataGraph" (and not bare "Strata" or "StrataCode")

A quick search of existing marks shows the unqualified word mark **"Strata" is
crowded and, in our exact category, likely contested.** That is why the name was
qualified. "StrataCode" was also rejected: an existing open-source dev tool
([stratacode.com](https://www.stratacode.com/), "type-safe layers of code") and a
UK company (STRATACODE LTD) already use it. "StrataGraph" creates distance and
describes the product (a cross-plane graph).

| Holder | Mark / use | Relevance |
|---|---|---|
| **Amazon Technologies, Inc.** | `STRATA` for "downloadable computer software for code review and analysis using automated reasoning" | **Direct overlap with our category.** The most serious conflict for the bare "Strata" mark: same field (code-analysis tooling). |
| **Strata Identity, Inc.** (`strata.io`) | Identity orchestration (IAM); now marketing AI-agent identity | Same root name, adjacent security/developer space; active and funded. |
| **Palo Alto Networks** | "Strata" network-security line (Strata Cloud Manager) | Same root name, different field (network security). |
| StrataCode (stratacode.com) + STRATACODE LTD (UK) | "Type-safe layers of code" dev tool; UK company | Why "StrataCode" was rejected: same name, same concept. |
| Stratagraph, Inc. | Oil & gas mud-logging services | Holds the word "Stratagraph" in a **different class** (geological services, not software). Lower risk; confirm in clearance. |
| Strata Decision Technology | Healthcare financial analytics | Different field. |

### Recommendation

"StrataGraph" is the chosen mark. Before any filing or major brand spend:

- Commission a **professional clearance search on "StrataGraph"** specifically,
  confirming the software / SaaS classes and the relationship (if any) to
  Stratagraph Inc.'s existing word mark in the geological-services class.
- Register the word mark, and reserve it for official builds (see the brand-use
  policy above).
- Keep "Strata" only as informal shorthand in prose, never as the legal brand.
